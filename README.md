This app will provide social interaction suggestions based on the human interaction that they pick of the time of day. For example: Morning will suggest "Send a Goodmorning text to a family member". Mid-Morning will suggest "Reach out to a colleague for a quick Thank You". Afternoon will suggest "Share a funny meme or interesting link with a friend". Afternoon Snack Time will suggest "Send a quick thinking of you message". Dinner will suggest "Call a friend or relative for a 5-minute catch-up". After Dinner will suggest "Leave a thoughtful comment on a friend's post". This app will help individuals quickly stay connected while trying to make it easy and enjoyable to use.
This app is pink made to make the user feel warm and welcomed. The style is curated to be able to help the user use it in an easy way so that no complications may come up.
